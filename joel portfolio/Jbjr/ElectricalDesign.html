Why Is Electrical Design Important

Electrical design is required to help save money in the long run. Opting for these services can help save on energy bills and extend equipment life by optimizing equipment usage.

Throughout the Philippines, the number of accidents caused by overload or short circuit has increased. Most of the time, these accidents are generated by poor and incorrect electrical installations.This is quite common in homes, mainly due to lack of information. Among the main causes of short circuits are the aging of the power grid, which is often old and needs maintenance. In addition, there is also the misuse of electrical outlets, which are overloaded due to the excessive number of equipment connected to it.

1. Floor Plan Analysis

The Construction System ideally, should be thought of during the design of Architectural, to prevent modifications are necessary in the original project, for example, to hide columns and beams. However, rarely is this what happens, so the choice of the type of structure also ends up being the responsibility of the Structural Designer.

2 . PTUE’S and Supply

Specific use points (PTUE’S) do not follow a specific standard. They depend on the device and its location. The determination of the minimum required is based on the sum of the active powers of all points.

3. Determination of electrical circuits

For safety reasons, circuits have an average of 10A (ampere) current. Thus, its maximum current may have only one circuit with a high power device, such as air conditioning. In addition, it is recommended to separate the lighting circuits from the socket circuits.

4. Check distribution board location

After determining the location of the switchboard, the power of consumption points is defined.

5. Electric Current

In order to determine the electric current mathematical formulas are used, avoiding circuits with more than 10 A.

6. Circuit Breaker Sizing Applied to Mediator Board

The type of circuit breaker depends on the type of supply and type of distribution system of the local utility company.

7. Electrical Point Rental Plant

After finalizing the power board, the electrical part is checked. In the rental plant there are all lighting points, general use and specific use points.

8. Single Line Diagram

Finally, the single-line diagram represents the trajectory of points and devices. The diagram shows the utility power, the mediation board, the distribution board, and the end circuits. The goal is to quickly check how many conductors will pass through certain conduits, in addition to their path.
