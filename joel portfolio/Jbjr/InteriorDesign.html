Inspiring Interior Design and Decoration

Interior design in the Philippines is not just an aesthetic factor, it is much more. We are talking about quality of life.

JBJR Construction Services Corporation is a company specializing in interior design projects for residential, corporate or real estate developments.

One of the great differentials of our work is the concern with details. The details make all the difference in an interior design. We pay attention to joinery, lighting, coverings, frames, baseboards, furniture, wallpapers, arts, vases, pillows, rugs, curtains, flowers and other items.
